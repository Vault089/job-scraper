## General Hiring Notes

**Work Visa Requirements (Vietnam):**
- Bachelor's degree (minimum)
- TESOL/TEFL/CELTA certification (preferred/required by most)
- 2+ years teaching experience (for some positions)
- Native English speaker preferred by many employers
- Criminal background check
- Medical certificate

**Salary Negotiation Points:**
- Housing allowances can add 5-15M VND/month value
- Contract completion bonuses often 1-2 months salary
- Flight reimbursement worth 15-30M VND annually
- Health insurance premium coverage varies

**Best Time to Apply:** January-March, August-September (school year transitions)

**Job Boards to Check:**
- Vietnamworks.com
- LinkedIn (search "English Teacher Vietnam")
- Dave's ESL Cafe
- ESL Tower
- Glassdoor Vietnam
